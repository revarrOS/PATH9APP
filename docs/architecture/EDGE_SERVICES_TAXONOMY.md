**Day 8-14: Connecting With Care Team**
- Medical Team Role Identifier (who does what)
- Communication Pattern Analyzer (extract communication preferences)

**Day 15-21: First Treatment Decision**
- Treatment Options Explainer (risks, benefits, alternatives)
- Decision Support Framework (preference alignment)

- Confidence Assessment (readiness to decide)

**Day 22-30: Building Routines**
- Medication Tracker with Adherence Insights
- Symptom Pattern Detector (correlations)
- Energy Level Forecaster
- Routine Optimizer (personalize based on patterns)

**Day 61-90: Milestone Tracking**
- Progress Marker (celebrate small wins)

- Goal Adjuster (recalibrate based on reality)

**Day 91-120: Long-term Planning**
- Follow-up Scheduler Intelligence

### Phase: CONTROL (Day 121-270)

## CROSS-JOURNEY SERVICES (Support All Pathways)

### Core Intelligence Services
- **User State Assessor** - Current phase, readiness, energy level
- **Context Aggregator** - Pull relevant data across all 5 pathways
- **Personalization Engine** - Adapt tone, complexity, pacing
- **Safety Monitor** - Flag medical emergencies, mental health crises
- **Progress Synthesizer** - Weekly/monthly summary generator
- **Goal Alignment Checker** - Ensure activities serve recovery goals
- **Intervention Trigger** - Detect when to escalate/adjust

### Conversational Intelligence (Gemma Core)
- **Intent Classifier** - What is user really asking for?
- **Empathy Generator** - Match emotional tone
- **Clarification Prompter** - Ask follow-ups when ambiguous
- **Memory Manager** - Recall past conversations
- **Continuity Maintainer** - Thread conversations over time

### Data Intelligence
- **Pattern Recognition Engine** - Cross-pathway insights
- **Anomaly Detector** - Flag unusual data points
- **Correlation Finder** - Connect behaviors to outcomes
- **Prediction Model** - Forecast needs, risks, opportunities

### Educational Intelligence
- **Knowledge Chunker** - Break complex topics into digestible pieces
- **Learning Path Optimizer** - Sequence education appropriately
- **Comprehension Checker** - Validate understanding
- **Resource Recommender** - Suggest articles, videos, communities
